Tomato soup

Every cold rainy-weather deserves a piping-hot tomato soup paired with a grilled cheese sandwich. Hard to go wrong kinda soup, just throw in some tomatoes in the blender and ta-da before u know it you have an amazing bowl of soup.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Basil leaves
  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken stock
